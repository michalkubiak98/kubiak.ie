## 1. Google Business Profile

1. Go to https://business.google.com
2. Sign in with Google Workspace (hi@kubiak.ie)
3. Click "Add your business"
4. Business name: **Kubiak**
5. Category: **"Web Designer"** (primary) - add secondary categories later like "Software Company", "Mobile App Developer"
6. Location: Choose **"No, I deliver goods and services to my customers"** and set service area to **Kerry, Ireland** (add Dublin, Wicklow, etc. later)
7. Contact: hi@kubiak.ie, website https://kubiak.ie
8. Verify: Google will send a postcard to your home address with a verification code OR offer phone/email verification. Postcard takes 5-7 days. Once verified, you show up on Google Maps.
9. After verification, fill out EVERYTHING:
   - Business description: "Software developer in Kerry, Ireland. I build websites, web apps, and mobile apps for local businesses. Quality work, at your budget."
   - Services: list each one (Website Design, Web App Development, Mobile App Development, SEO)
   - Hours: set your working hours
   - Photos: add a screenshot of kubiak.ie, maybe the TimelessMoments site
   - Posts: Google lets you make posts (like mini blog updates) - do one a week

## 2. Get a review from TimelessMoments

Once your Google Business Profile is verified:

1. Go to your Business Profile
2. Click "Ask for reviews" - it gives you a direct link
3. Send that link to your TimelessMoments client
4. Ask them to write something genuine like "Michael built our website from scratch. Professional, fast, and great communication."
5. Even 1 review puts you ahead of competitors with zero reviews in the map pack
6. Respond to the review publicly - Google likes active business owners

## 3. Service pages

Right now kubiak.ie is a single-page site. Google sees it as one page about everything. When someone searches "web design kerry", Google prefers a page specifically about web design in Kerry.

Add routes (React Router) and create dedicated pages:

- **kubiak.ie/web-design-kerry** - "Website Design in Kerry" page
  - What you build, your process, pricing ballpark, CTA to contact
  - Target keywords: "web design kerry", "website design tralee", "web designer kerry"
- **kubiak.ie/mobile-apps-kerry** - "Mobile App Development in Kerry"
  - iOS/Android, React Native expertise, process
- **kubiak.ie/dashboards** - "Custom Dashboards & Internal Tools"
  - Enterprise experience, what you build
- **kubiak.ie/seo-kerry** - "SEO Services in Kerry"

Each page should have:
- Unique `<title>` and `<meta description>`
- H1 with the target keyword naturally included
- 300-500 words of genuine content
- A CTA linking to your contact section
